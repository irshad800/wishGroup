Terragrove - The Earth Zone (5 acres)

Theme: Sustainability, Calm & Reflection

Botanical Gardens

Immerse yourself in our curated collection of native and exotic plants, showcasing sustainable gardening practices and biodiversity conservation.

Eco-Pathways & Landscaping Exhibits

Walk through interactive exhibits demonstrating sustainable landscaping techniques, water conservation, and permaculture principles.

Agriculture Learning Hub

Hands-on experiences with urban farming techniques, hydroponics, and organic gardening for all ages.

Sustainability Workshops

Daily workshops on composting, renewable energy, and sustainable living practices for the whole family.

Specialized programs using horse interaction to promote emotional growth and personal development.

Meditation Gardens
