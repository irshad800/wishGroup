Wish Holdings Pvt Ltd

WHO WE ARE

Wish Holdings Pvt Ltd is a Maldivian-based company operating under the global umbrella of Wish Group UAE. Established with a commitment to sustainability, innovation, and community development, Wish Holdings serves as the flagship entity leading transformative initiatives across the Indian Ocean. With a strong foundation in ethical practices and future-driven thinking, Wish Holdings is dedicated to elevating the Maldives as a global hub for premium seafood production, sustainable infrastructure, and responsible tourism.

Wish Holdings – Vision

To lead the Indian Ocean region in sustainable, ocean-driven innovations by transforming the Maldives into a global hub for premium seafood, eco-tourism, and ethical investment.

Wish Holdings – Mission

  • To develop world-class eco-conscious infrastructure that uplifts communities and respects marine ecosystems.
  • To operate cutting-edge seafood production and luxury hospitality projects that reflect environmental stewardship and economic empowerment.
  • To build global trust through transparent partnerships and high-quality Maldivian exports.
  • To position Wish Holdings as the leading enterprise of the Wish Group, integrating sustainability, innovation, and social impact into every venture.

Wish Group – UAE

WISH GROUP is a global enterprise operating across 7 countries with 33 companies under its umbrella, spanning Investments, Projects, and Trading. Backed by strong partnerships between local entrepreneurs and international investors, the group is advancing 4 Mega Projects and planning 10 landmark initiatives worldwide. Diversified and dynamic, WISH GROUP offers high-return opportunities across multiple sectors — far beyond any single industry focus.

WHAT WE DO

Fishing

We use sustainable pole-and-line fishing to selectively harvest premium-grade tuna, preserving marine ecosystems and reducing environmental impact.

Farming

We cultivate chemical-free prawns in clean, eco-friendly environments, blending tradition with modern sustainable practices.

Manufacturing

Our advanced seafood facilities ensure hygienic processing, efficient output, and consistent quality to meet global food safety standards.

Processing

Our expert team processes and packages seafood with precision, meeting HACCP and ISO standards for global market quality.

Exporting

We ensure fast, fresh, and traceable seafood exports through efficient logistics and trusted global trade networks.

Marketing

Our marketing team builds global demand through digital outreach, sustainability storytelling, and premium branding.

Sustainability Process

At Wish Holdings, eco-consciousness is at the heart of every venture we undertake. From renewable energy integration in our mega projects to sustainable fishing, ethical farming, and waste-minimizing production, we embed environmental responsibility into our business model.

By aligning with global sustainability standards and investing in green innovation, we ensure that our growth supports both people and the planet — now and for the future.
