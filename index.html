Wish Group

Wish Group, founded in 2000 in the UAE, is a purpose-driven global conglomerate headquartered in Dubai’s Business Bay. Operating across 7+ countries, it spans sectors like real estate, hospitality, seafood, trading, and capital ventures. Guided by the vision of “One Capital World,” Wish Group connects capital with compassion through sustainable, inclusive, and innovative development. Its core mission is to empower people and shape futures by uniting investment with impact.
