Project Timeline

Key phases of development

2019-2020

Planning and design phase with RSP architects. Initial land acquisition and approvals secured.

2020

Estimated annual investment: $445 million. Commencement of foundation work and initial construction.

2021

Estimated annual investment: $450 million. Vertical construction begins with core structure development.

Final construction phase with interior fit-outs and systems commissioning. Preparation for grand opening.
