Our History – From Dream to Global Force

The journey of Wish Group began with a single vision in 2012.

What started as a boutique concept in Maldives and Sri Lanka has grown into a multinational organization.

2012: Conceptualization in Sri Lanka
The seed of Wish Group was planted, with a vision to create a unique lifestyle brand blending innovation, ethics, and global ambition.
2014: First trading operations begin under Wish Brand
Our trading division launched, marking the first step in turning our vision into reality.
2017: Expansion into UAE & official registration of Wish Group
The UAE became our new base of operations, solidifying our presence in the Middle East market.
2019: Launch of real estate and investment projects
We ventured into luxury real estate and high-value investment opportunities, expanding our portfolio.
2021: Initiation of floating villa innovation & Wish Island Project
Our pioneering floating villa concept and the grand Wish Island Project began, redefining luxury living.
2023: Launch of Wish I Club, Prime Bond, and entry into Europe
We introduced exclusive membership programs and investment platforms while expanding into European markets.
2025: 20+ registered companies, cross-continental partnerships, and luxury CSR projects
Today, we stand as a global force, committed to excellence, partnerships, and impactful social responsibility.
Every year, every move — aligned with a higher purpose.
